Order & Return Policy

We want you to have the best experience shopping with us. Please review our order and return policies below.

1. Placing an Order

Orders can be placed online at any time. Once an order is placed, you will receive a confirmation email. Processing typically takes 1–2 business days.

2. Shipping

Shipping times vary depending on your location. Standard delivery within the country takes 2–5 business days. International shipping may take longer.

3. Order Changes

If you need to change or cancel your order, please contact us within 12 hours of placing it. After that, we may not be able to guarantee changes.

4. Return Eligibility

Returns are accepted within 14 days of delivery. Items must be unused, in original packaging, and accompanied by a receipt or proof of purchase.

5. Non-Returnable Items

Certain types of items such as perishable goods (e.g., loose leaf teas), custom orders, or personal care items cannot be returned.

6. Refunds

Once your return is received and inspected, we will notify you via email. If approved, your refund will be processed within 5–10 business days.

7. Contact Us

For any questions regarding your order or return, feel free to contact our support team at support@example.com.
